Reaching for the Hamsters

Posted by Song Chien on June 4th, 2021

“If the sun were made of hamsters, the earth would be incinerated.” – Michael Schumacher


Hamsters are rodents belonging to the subfamily Cricetinae, which contains 19 species classified in seven genera.

Hamsters are the most common first pet for children. Living to around 2 years, they’re another small mammal that are a great way to introduce caring for animals into your children’s lives. With a few different breeds, it’s best to go to a licensed supplier to make sure you know exactly what you’re buying. Some breeds are very sociable, but others prefer to live alone.

Besides food and water, hamsters need plenty of bedding and nesting material. On top of that, they require cautious handling as they are very delicate, and are also nocturnal so that must be beared in mind.
