Reaching for the Cats

Posted by Cong Pai on June 24, 2021

“A happy arrangement: many people prefer cats to other people, and many cats prefer people to other cats.” – Mason Cooley


The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae

The other top dog when it comes to the nation’s favourite pet has to be the cat! These fluffy little critters are amazing house pets. They’re a fairly big commitment too, but do take less work than dogs. Depending on where you live you can opt to keep your cat indoors, or you can install a cat flap and let them roam to their heart’s content.

Ensure your cat is fully vaccinated, microchipped and knows where you live – so if you recently moved or your cat is still young, you should probably keep an eye on them. If not, then a litter tray placed in a safe and quiet place is all you need. Plus food and lots of affection, that is.
